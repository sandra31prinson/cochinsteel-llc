About Cochin Steel LLC

Cochin Steel LLC. is one of the reputed companies engaged in the fabrication and installation of storage tanks for Diesel, Petrol and Water tanks. We are also specialists in the manufacturing of water storage solutions for fire water as well as irrigation water.

Best solutions are our Zinc Alum bolted tanks as well as our GLS/ GFS bolted tanks. We also undertake customized manufacturing services like Oxy Fuel Cutting, Plate Rolling, Dish end manufacturing, MIG Welding, Arc Welding, Machine Shop Works etc.

Based in UAE, Cochin Steel is a market leader and pioneer in this industry and have been serving our esteemed clients with top of the line storage tank solutions and services.
