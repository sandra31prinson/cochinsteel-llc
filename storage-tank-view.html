Petrol Storage Tank

Cochin Steel LLC is a prominent player in the manufacturing and marketing of a wide range of Self Bunded and Non-Bunded Fuel Storage Tanks, specifically designed for the safe storage and efficient dispensing of Petrol (Gasoline). Our specialized range of Petrol storage tanks varies in size from 500L to an impressive 250,000L capacity, catering to diverse industrial requirements in the UAE.

Petrol, being a highly flammable and combustible substance, requires utmost care in handling and storage. It has the potential to produce vapors that can easily ignite, leading to a serious fire or explosion if not managed safely. At Cochin Steel, safety is our priority, and we ensure that our Petrol storage tanks comply with the highest safety standards to minimize risks and protect both your business and surroundings.

With years of expertise in the field, our team of experts excels in customizing fuel storage solutions to meet your specific needs. Share the details of your storage tank application, and we will design, develop, manufacture, and install an intelligent and tailored solution that adds substantial value to your operations.

What sets us apart is our in-depth knowledge and experience in catering to the unique requirements of the oil and gas industry. Our fuel storage tanks are engineered to meet the demanding specifications of major players in the UAE, including ADNOC specs, DEWA/Dubai Municipality specs/ADCD Specs, and ENOC/EMARAT specs. We understand the critical role of reliable and safe fuel storage in the oil field operations, and our tanks are designed to withstand the challenging environments and ensure uninterrupted supply.

Moreover, Cochin Steel is firmly rooted in the Emirates of UAE, making us well-versed in the local regulations and industry standards. Our manufacturing facilities utilize state-of-the-art technologies and adhere to strict quality control measures to produce top-quality fuel storage tanks that meet and exceed client expectations.

We take pride in our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. As a trusted and reputed manufacturer in the UAE, we have successfully served numerous esteemed clients since 2006. Whether you are looking for Petrol storage solutions for a small-scale project or a large-scale oil field operation, Cochin Steel is your reliable partner to deliver customized fuel storage tanks that not only meet stringent safety requirements but also enhance the efficiency and productivity of your operations.

Choose Cochin Steel LLC for your Petrol storage needs and experience the assurance of working with a market leader and pioneer in the industry. Contact us today to discuss your requirements and explore how our expertise can fuel your success in the oil and gas sector in the Emirates of UAE.
